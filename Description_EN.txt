[b]Russian translation for The Old Realms mods[/b]

Translates [b]The Old Realms — Core[/b] and [b]The Old Realms — Armory[/b] into Russian. Ships as a standalone module that loads after TOR_Core and overrides its localization without modifying the original mod files. This means the translation survives TOR updates — just update this module separately.

Compatibility: TOR_Core v1.3.15 / TOR_Armory v1.3.15.

[h1]What's included[/h1]
[list]
[*] 33 localization files — about 17 000 translated strings
[*] Overridden GUI prefabs: inventory tooltips, spellbook, battle prayer book, enchanting screen
[*] Consistent Warhammer Fantasy terminology (Grungni, Laurelorn, Sylvania, Mousillon, Skaven, Warboss, Greenskins, Orc-Boy, Reiksguard, Teef, Winds of Magic, etc.)
[*] Mass fixes of broken transliteration, grammatical cases, gender agreement, truncated sentences
[*] Proofread biographies, career descriptions, perks, prayers, spells, troop names, dialogues
[/list]

[h1]Installation[/h1]
[list]
[*] Subscribe to this module on the Workshop
[*] Make sure you have [b]TOR_Core[/b] installed (required) and preferably [b]TOR_Armory[/b]
[*] In the Bannerlord launcher enable [b]The Old Realms — Russian Translation[/b]. It must be [b]below[/b] TOR_Core in the load order
[*] Set game language to [b]Русский[/b] in game settings
[/list]

[h1]Issues and feedback[/h1]
If you see an untranslated string, strange word form or broken sentence — leave a comment with a screenshot if possible. Some English labels may remain in rare spots — those are either not yet localized or hardcoded in the mod's DLL.

[h1]Notes[/h1]
Text-only translation. Voice-over, videos and other assets are unchanged. Ink quests (book-based side stories) temporarily remain in English due to compatibility issues — they will be added in future updates.
